Usage Guide

Simple Steps,
Powerful Results

Follow these easy steps for maximum benefit from your Ayurvedic formulations.

01
Prepare the Pack
Take the required amount of Keshvardhini powder in a clean bowl. Add plain water, rose water, or lukewarm water gradually to form a smooth, lump-free paste.
02
Section & Apply
Part your hair into sections. Apply the paste directly onto the scalp and hair roots using your fingertips, massaging gently in circular motions.
03
Rest & Let Work
Cover your hair with a shower cap. Leave the pack on for 30–45 minutes. The warmth helps the herbs penetrate deeply into the scalp and hair shaft.
04
Rinse & Repeat
Wash off thoroughly with lukewarm water and a mild natural shampoo. Use 2–3 times a week for best results. You will see visible improvement within 7 days.
💡
Dr. Bhumika's Tip
For best results with dandruff, apply the pack on dry hair (not wet) and add a few drops of coconut oil to the paste. Avoid using hot water to rinse — lukewarm water preserves the herb benefits.
01
Cleanse First
Wash your face, hands, or the target area gently with cold or lukewarm water. Pat dry softly with a clean, soft cotton towel. Do not rub.
02
Take a Small Amount
Using a clean spatula or the back of your fingernail, scoop a pea-sized amount of Shatdhaut Ghrita. A little goes a very long way with this formulation.
03
Warm & Apply
Warm the Ghrita between your fingertips for 5 seconds. Apply on face, lips, dark spots, or cracked heels using gentle upward circular massaging movements.
04
Use Daily
Apply every morning and before bed at night. Works best on slightly damp skin for deeper absorption. Consistent use over 2–3 weeks shows dramatic results.
💡
Dr. Bhumika's Tip
For cracked heels, apply a generous amount of Shatdhaut Ghrita at night and wear cotton socks. You will notice a huge difference in just 3–5 days. For lips, apply every night before sleeping for soft, nourished lips.

Are AyurCare products completely safe? ▾
Yes, absolutely. All AyurCare products are formulated by Dr. Bhumika Kirote, a qualified BAMS physician, using only natural Ayurvedic herbs. They contain zero synthetic chemicals, preservatives, or artificial fragrances — making them safe for all skin and hair types, including sensitive skin.
How soon can I expect results? ▾
Most customers notice visible improvement within 7 days of regular use. For the Keshvardhini Hair Pack, dandruff reduction is often seen after just 2–3 applications. For Shatdhaut Ghrita, cracked heels respond very quickly (3–5 days), while skin brightening shows gradual improvement over 2–3 weeks of consistent use.
Can I use both products together? ▾
Yes! Many of our customers use both products as part of their holistic Ayurvedic care routine. Keshvardhini is for hair and scalp care, while Shatdhaut Ghrita is for skin, lips, and heels. They complement each other beautifully and there is no interaction between them.
Is Shatdhaut Ghrita suitable for oily skin? ▾
Shatdhaut Ghrita, when processed through the 100-wash method, becomes extremely light and non-greasy. It absorbs quickly into the skin without clogging pores. However, for very oily skin types, we recommend starting with a tiny amount and observing how your skin responds. Dr. Bhumika can provide personalized guidance via WhatsApp.
